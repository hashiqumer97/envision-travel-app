Welcome to Travel Me 360

The Best Travel Agency

As a country blessed with incomparable natural charm, as well a rich cultural heritage spanning nearly 3 millennia, Sri Lanka possesses immense potential for inbound tourism. With a view to tapping this lucrative segment, Traveller Global Destinations & Leisure Holidays (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated in 2002, as a subsidiary of The Traveller Global (Pvt) Ltd. Our parent company was formed by Chairman/CEO, Mr. Mohamed Rizmi Reyal, who hails from an established business family in Kandy and counts over 20 years’ in the travel & tourism industry. The Traveller Global (Pvt) Ltd is currently Sri Lanka’s largest outbound travel operator, counting foreign Embassies, NGOs, blue-chip corporates, Recruitment Agencies and leading personages among its established clientele. Our Company, Traveller Global Destinations & Leisure Holidays (Pvt) Ltd, functions under the stewardship of Mr. Ramzi Reyal, who is a respected and well-known personality in the travel & tourism industry. His ‘Hands-on’ approach and personal involvement in all aspects of day-to-day operations is an asset to the company.
